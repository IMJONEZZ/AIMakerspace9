## üöÄ Getting Started

### Q: How do I start using AI Life Coach?
**A**: Getting started is simple:

1. **Download and Install**: Visit our website and download the app for your platform
2. **Create Account**: Sign up with your email and create a secure password
3. **Initial Assessment**: Complete the comprehensive 20-30 minute life assessment
4. **Set Your First Goals**: Work with your coach to identify 3-5 priority goals
5. **Begin Your Journey**: Start with daily check-ins and weekly planning sessions

**Pro Tip**: Schedule uninterrupted time for your initial assessment - this foundation session sets up your entire coaching relationship.

### Q: How much time do I need to commit?
**A**: AI Life Coach is designed to fit your schedule:

- **Daily Check-ins**: 5-10 minutes
- **Weekly Reviews**: 15-20 minutes  
- **Monthly Deep Dives**: 30-45 minutes
- **Quarterly Assessments**: 60-90 minutes

**Minimum Effective Usage**: 3-4 days per week for noticeable progress
**Optimal Usage**: Daily engagement for maximum transformation

### Q: What happens in the first session?
**A**: Your first session includes:

1. **Welcome Introduction**: Meet your AI coach and understand the approach
2. **Comprehensive Assessment**: Deep dive into all life domains
3. **Values Clarification**: Identify what matters most to you
4. **Goal Discovery**: Explore areas for growth and improvement
5. **Planning Overview**: Understand the coaching framework
6. **Next Steps**: Set up your personalized coaching journey

You'll leave with a clear understanding of your current situation and initial priorities.

### Q: Do I need to prepare anything?
**A**: Minimal preparation needed:

- **Open Mindset**: Be honest and open about your life situation
- **Quiet Space**: Find a comfortable, distraction-free environment
- **20-30 Minutes**: Allow enough time for the initial assessment
- **Honesty**: Share your real feelings and challenges

No need to prepare documents or complete any forms beforehand.

### Q: What is goal dependency mapping?
**A**: Goal dependency mapping shows how your goals connect and affect each other:

**How It Works**:
- Analyzes relationships between your goals
- Identifies enabling, required, supporting, and conflicting relationships
- Creates visual maps of goal interdependencies
- Optimizes goal sequencing and timing
- Prevents conflicts and resource competition

**Example Dependencies**:
- **Enabling**: "Get promotion" enables "Buy house"
- **Required**: "Run marathon" requires "6 months training"
- **Supporting**: "Exercise regularly" supports "Better sleep"
- **Conflicting**: "Work 60 hours/week" conflicts with "Family time"

**Benefits**:

- Identifies potential conflicts before they cause problems
- Creates synergistic goals that support each other
- Helps prioritize limited resources (time, money, energy)
